Meet Our Science Committee

"I'm no longer having
that stress inside"

Baunda is a Congolese refugee living in Uganda. He came to Uganda after his brother was killed in his house.

Before he learned TM, Baunda says that the pressure he experienced was taking away his heart and that his heart was not settled.

Since learning TM, Bunda told us, "The meditation (Transcendental Meditation) is helping me a lot because I’m no longer having that stress inside of me as I was having before.... Now I feel like I'm really settled inside me and I feel more hopeful."
